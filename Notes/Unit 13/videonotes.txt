Conventional Loans 
    No government involvement 
    1.) Debt repayment is based soley on the borrorwer's ability to repay. 
        a) Loans are not insured or guaranteed. 
    2.) Maximum LTV is 80%. 

Insured Conventional Mortgages 
    1.) Private mortgage insurance (PMI) protects lenders from loss due to a deficiency after foreclosure. 
    2.) A borrower/mortgagor pays PMI when the LTV is above 80%.
        a) PMI does not pay off the full loan. 
         (1) PMI covers a portion of the shortfall between the amount obtained through the REO sale and the outstanding mortgage debt.
        b) The advantage for the brrower is a lower down payment.
