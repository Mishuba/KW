Conducting the Closing 
    Agent is conductor of the closing bringing all they key players together to make sure the title passes at the closing. 

    Face to Face Closing 
        Held at the office of the 
            Title company
            Lending Institution 
            Attorney for One of the parties
            Real Estate Professional 
            County Recorder 
            Escrow Company 

    Escrow Closing
        A disinterested third party is authorized to act as an escrow agent or escrow holder and coordinate the closing activity. 

    The real estate professional role at closing can vary from simply collecting the commission to conducting the proceedings. 
    Although they do not always conduct the closing proceeding, they ususally attednd whether representing the buyer or seller
